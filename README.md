## 🌟 What is ON-YOU Flow?

ON-YOU Flow is an **AI-powered spiritual companion app** that listens to the specific context of a person's pain — and responds with the perfect scripture and music to rekindle their soul.

When someone types *"I failed my job interview and feel worthless"* —  
ON-YOU Flow doesn't return a generic list.  
It reads between the lines, understands the **shame and anxiety**, and curates a deeply personal response.

> **"Not a vending machine. A private pastry chef for the soul."**

---

## 💡 Core Features

### 🎧 AI Vibe Listening
- Powered by **Gemini 2.5 Flash**
- Understands emotional subtext, not just keywords
- Detects the specific pain hidden beneath the words

### 📖 Spiritual Prescription
- Matches curated **Bible verses** to the exact emotional context
- Pairs with **2AM with you** — original healing music IP
- Where the frequency of music meets the power of the Word

### 🟠 Orange Dot — Shared Grace
- Users anonymously post prayer requests
- Others light an **Orange Dot** (not a "like" — a *prayer*)
- Watch the dots multiply: *"Someone is praying for me right now"*
- Visual solidarity in the darkest hours
